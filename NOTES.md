Heads-up: the real record uses a richer schema
The original uses fields my current types.ts doesn't have yet:

Organ: suburb, lat, lng, nominalPitchHz, pitchRefTempC, baseTuneMinutes, keyNumber, thermalMass, accessNotes, standingNotes, reviewed (boolean, not my 'pending'|'approved').
Builder: work (I called it role).
Division: chestType, windPressure, chestOriginal, chestProvenance.
Stop: name embeds the pitch (e.g. "Bourdon 16"), plus original + provenance — my model splits name/pitch and has no provenance.
Visit: durationMinutes, tempC, tempSite, humidity, pitchHz, workDone, technician, unresolved, resolved — much richer than my tunedCount/notes.
